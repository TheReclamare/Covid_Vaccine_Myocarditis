Up to 20 Million Doses Were Administered After Health Canada Received a Myocarditis Warning, and Before the Public Was Told

31,659 adverse event reports of myocarditis and pericarditis following COVID-19 vaccination, disclosed through Written Question Q-766 in the House of Commons. Click each question to mark it on the timeline.

Context: COVID-19 vaccines are effective and have saved lives. This analysis examines one question: whether Canadians received timely information about a known cardiac risk — the standard applied to every other vaccine in Canada's history.
